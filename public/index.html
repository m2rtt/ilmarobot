Privacy policy

What kind of personal information is being collected?

Our application does not collect any personal information. Persons information will never get out of Facebook.

When do we collect it?

We do not collect any information. Facebook itself gives us persons name and e-mail, but we do not use this.

What do we do with personal information?

Absolutely nothing. This application is only for serving information based on user input. We only respond to messages, never initate a conversation.

How is personal information protected?

Personal information never gets out of Facebook so protection is as good as Facebook's user protection gets.
Also our application is hosted on a secured network using https protocol.

Do we use cookies?

No.

What about third party

We do not pass any personal information to third party
